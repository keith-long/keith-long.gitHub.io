The Story of The Molecule

The fall os 1994 was a quixotic time for me, The Mad Prophet of the Apocalypse. Turner Broadcasting here in Atlanta, Georgia, USA, produced a movie about the Civil War, but instead of being pro-Confederacy, it was about a southern prison camp called Andersonville and it's mistreatment of Union soldiers. For the first time I had the opportunity to be with about 60 of my closest friends getting paid to dress up and act! It was great. Since the shots took so incredibly long to set up, and centered mostly around the actions of the primary actors, we spent long periods of time grouped together in the background. A true circus! It was Abe Burgess at this point who discovered the cry which united us each and every day. "Deviants Come!", I would cry, and Abe commented that we were like a big Deviant Molecule. So there you have it. The Deviant Molecule is both a personal fantasy and something real. It is a community of people who are artists and aspiring artists, that have each shared something mystical and magical with me. This page is dedicated to all those people who have, are, and will be a part of The Molecule. It hopefully will portray this community through it's works of art in a similar way that musical circles like Tricky-Portishead-Primal Scream or The Legendary Pink Dots-Skinny Puppy-Nurse With Wound-Current 93 are portrayed in their own genres.
My life is devoted to musical pursuits, and as such I create lyrics that run parallel to events in my life, yet are Mythical Stories at the same time, and all my friends are in there somewhere! Deviant Atoms are invited to submit Poems and other Artwork to this site in the hopes that a greater Vision can be percieved about our lives through the artwork of the group. News about events in the lives of the Deviants will be available, as well as a page of Pictures and Bios (all completely true of course!) where you can meet the Deviants themselves! Eventually, my hopes center around adapting this page to being the official page for my musical endeavors, with a discography etc., so cross your fingers because I believe that each and every one of us WILL SEE OUR KINGDOMS COME, if we can only stick together. How to join The Molecule? Take my hand and hold it.....

Much Love and Peace-
The MadProphet
